Our Community

  To support and promote Lane Tech's Varsity cheer program by fostering school spirit, encouraging community involvement, and providing financial and volunteer resources that empower our cheerleaders to thrive—on and off the sidelines.

Our Community

Our Community

Our Community

As a 501(c)3, we are proud to be a part of the Chicago community and to have the opportunity to give back through our various outreach programs. Our athletes and coaches are actively involved in volunteering, fundraising, and community events, and we are committed to making a positive impact in our city.

Our Support

Funds that we raise are used to defray costs of uniforms, training programs, transportation, team gatherings, and coach/athlete recognition.
